Foods for Healthy Skin

Recommended foods based on your dietary preference

Protein Sources

  • Fatty fish (salmon, mackerel)
  • Chicken
  • Eggs
  • Lean beef
  • Turkey

Collagen-Supporting Foods

  • Bone broth
  • Chicken skin
  • Fish with skin
  • Beef
  • Pork

Skin Superfoods

  • Berries
  • Nuts
  • Avocados
  • Fatty fish
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil

Antioxidant-Rich Foods

Fight free radicals and reduce inflammation

  • Blueberries, strawberries, and other berries
  • Dark chocolate (70%+ cocoa)
  • Leafy greens like spinach and kale
  • Beans, peas, and lentils

Vitamin-Rich Foods

Support skin health and regeneration

  • Citrus fruits (vitamin C)
  • Sweet potatoes (vitamin A)
  • Nuts and seeds (vitamin E)
  • Mushrooms (vitamin D)

Inflammatory Foods

Can trigger skin issues and accelerate aging

  • Processed foods with artificial ingredients
  • Refined carbohydrates and sugars
  • Fried foods and trans fats
  • Excessive alcohol

Common Trigger Foods

May cause breakouts or skin reactions

  • Dairy products (for some people)
  • High-glycemic foods
  • Chocolate (for some individuals)
  • Spicy foods (can trigger rosacea)
