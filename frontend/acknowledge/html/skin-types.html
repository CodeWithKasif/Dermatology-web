Skin Types

Understanding your skin type is the first step to proper skin care

What's Your Skin Type?

Everyone's skin is unique, but there are some common skin types that can help you understand your skin's needs better. Knowing your skin type can help you choose the right products and develop an effective skincare routine.

Common Skin Types

  • Balanced oil production
  • Few imperfections
  • Not too dry or too oily
  • Small pores
  • Radiant complexion

Care Tips:

  • Gentle cleansing twice daily
  • Regular moisturizing
  • Sun protection
  • Mild exfoliation 1-2 times per week

  • Almost invisible pores
  • Dull, rough complexion

  • Visible lines
  • Flaking, scaling, or peeling

Care Tips:

  • Gentle, hydrating cleansers
  • Rich moisturizers
  • Hydrating masks

  • Use a humidifier
  • Gentle exfoliation

  • Enlarged pores
  • Shiny, thick complexion
  • Blackheads, pimples, or other blemishes

  • Regular exfoliation
  • Oil-free moisturizers
  • Clay masks
  • Blotting papers

How to Determine Your Skin Type

The Bare-Face Method:

  1. Cleanse your face with a mild cleanser and gently pat dry.
  2. Wait 30 minutes without applying any products.
  3. Observe how your skin feels and looks:
    • Tight and flaky: Dry skin
    • Shiny all over: Oily skin
    • Shiny T-zone, normal/dry elsewhere: Combination skin
    • Comfortable, not too oily or dry: Normal skin
    • Red, itchy, or burning: Sensitive skin
